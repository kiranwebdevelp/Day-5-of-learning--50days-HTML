Lists in HTML

Unordered Lists with ul and li

Note: Unordered lists are used to group a collection of items that do not have a specific order or sequence.

Example:

  • John Doe
  • To Kill a Mockingbird
  • New York City

Unordered List Example:

  • The Beatles
  • Star Wars
  • Pizza Hut

Ordered Lists with ol and li

Note: Ordered lists are used to group a collection of items that have a specific order or sequence.

Example:

  1. Gold Medal
  2. Silver Medal
  3. Bronze Medal

Ordered List Example:
  1. iPhone 13
  2. Samsung Galaxy S22
  3. Microsoft Surface Laptop

Nesting Lists

Note: Lists can be nested inside each other to create a hierarchical structure.

Example:

  • Documents
    • Resume.pdf
    • Cover Letter.docx
  • Images
    • profile.jpg
    • background.png

Nested List Example:

  • Harvard University
    • Bachelor's Degree
    • Master's Degree
